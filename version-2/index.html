I'm looking to get professional help

We encourage people wanting to access our services to contact their GP. If you do not have a GP, we strongly encourage you to visit one and engage regularly with a GP.

We work alongside people to help them recover from a mental health illness. The most effective pathway to recovery usually involves a combination of medical, social and psychological supports.
